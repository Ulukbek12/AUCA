AUCA Virtual Museum

Over the past 30 incredible years, AUCA has become an indispensable and familiar
institution throughout Central Asia as it continues to nurture and develop leaders to
gradually transform the region into a better place. Every year the history of AUCA is filled
with new significant events and people who somehow left a mark on the history of the
university. To capture all the iconic moments in digital format we designed the
"Virtual Museum" which will be a reflection of the past, present, and future of our university

We invite you to explore the history and current state of innovation at the AUCA through the
Virtual Museum which recognizes the vision and contribution of all those who made AUCA
the best university in Central Asia and provides everyone with the opportunity to make a
small contribution to the future home of AUCA, as it opens the doors for the next 30 years

The AUCA Virtual Museum tells the story of AUCA in five exhibition sections, which are
presented in detail below:
The Beginning: Humanities in Central Asia
Students
Graduates
Partnership of Bards
Future

Each section reflects one aspect of what makes AUCA unique: the initial energy and passion
of the Kyrgyz-American faculty; talented and creative students; fearless and successful
graduates; the investment and integrity of Bard College at AUCA;and, finally, AUCA's
commitment to leading innovation and change both in the region and around the world.

AUCA would like to thank all its local and international supporters for making AUCA what it is
today, especially the Open Society Foundations and the United States Agency for International
Development.

The AUCA Virtual Museum is a forum where the AUCA community can share photos, videos,
and memories of their stay in AUCA. New materials can be sent by email to pr@auca.kg.
We encourage all graduates, teachers, and friends to send their materials.
