The New Star

History Of The New Star

Officially, the American University of Central Asia first published an independent student newspaper in October 1999 under the name AUK Student Newspaper; a month later, the second issue was releasedunder the title The Star. In 2008, the newspaper was renamed The New Star. This newspaper provides a platform for independent student writing, discussing important campus events, student success stories, and activities both on and off-campus. The New Star does not serve the administration or aim to promote the university; rather, it offers students the opportunity to voice their ideas, concerns, and desires. After a prolonged hiatus, The New Star returned to print and began publishing online on November 22, 2020.

Prize

"The New Star" newspaper won the "Best Student Newspaper 2008" award at the Annual National Newspaper Prize contest organized by the Journalism Association of Kyrgyzstan.

Format

Narrative articles are based on suggested topics or those offered by students, confirmed via email. There is a possibility to submit a draft of 300-500 words and to consult either in person or through email. Every student can take advantage of this opportunity and share their writings without any hesitation. The only requirements are to ensure that articles are your own work, do not violate university rules, harm any gender, or relate to politics.
