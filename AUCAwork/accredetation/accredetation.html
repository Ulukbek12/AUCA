Accreditation in the Kyrgyz Republic

The educational activities of the University are carried out on the basis of the legal
acts of the Kyrgyz Republic in education, on the basis of the Charter of the
University,and on the basis of the licenses given by the Ministry of Education
and Science in the area of higher education.
