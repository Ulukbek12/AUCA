Why Support Auca

Support AUCA today, live in a better world tomorrow!

AUCA is the best university in the Central Asian region thanks to the students, faculty, alumni, and donors like you who make up the AUCA community. Your support helps AUCA move forward in providing the highest quality liberal arts education in Central Asia and educating impassioned leaders for the democratic transformation of the region

Your gift to AUCA matters and makes a difference to the lives of future generations of students and beyond. Our research activities bring enlightenment to Central Asian studies, our civic engagement has a positive impact on many lives and surrounding ecosystems, our alumni are successful leaders contributing to economic growth by providing job opportunities and generating innovative solutions to the challenges of our region. By giving to AUCA, you will strengthen our capacity to sustain and further advance our learning, teaching, research and civic engagement.
